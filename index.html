About me

I’m a Computer Science student with a Biochemistry concentration at Carleton College (Class of 2027). In addition to my courseload, I currently work as a Carleton prefect in the Chemistry department, and as a lab assistant in the Computer Science department. I am working towards a career in medicine, and aiming to attend medical school.

Some of my honors and awards include being a National Merit Scholarship recipient, a Gilman Scholar, and an Eagle Scout. I volunteer with the Food Recovery Network at Carleton and the Ronald McDonald House in Rochester. I have a combined 60+ hours shadowing Nephrologist Dr. John Dillon and the Mayo Clinic ICU Nephrology and Hypertension team, as well as Radiation Oncologists Dr. Meng Welliver, Dr. Mark Waddle, and Dr. Sean Park in the Mayo Clinic Radiation Oncology Department.
